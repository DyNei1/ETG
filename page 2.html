GUNGEON PROPER

The Ammoconda goes around the room chasing the player. The Ammoconda is an enemy built from segments and these segments do the attack:
♦ Firing bullets in random direction out.
♦ Fires bullets in many directions.
♦ Fires bullets in a slithering pattern.
♦ Fires bullets which make sharp turns towards the player.
♦ Stops attacking for couple of seconds after which every segment fires bursts of bullets.

The Beholster is a stationary enemy that attacks in all directions but does not move. His attacks are:
♦ Firing a spread of bullets, followed up by ring of bullets.
♦ Fires ultra fast small beams of light towards the player.
♦ Fires Heatseeking missles in a random direction which chase the player, but this missles can be destroyed.
♦ Charges up his eye after which The Beholster shoots a continuous beam of light, which goes back and forth towards the player.
♦ Spawns his minions which look like small versions of him and shoot small moving bullets.

The Gorgun is one of the hardest bosses in the game it has a stationary state and a moving state. Her attacks are:
♦ Creates a shockwave and if the player is hit the player won't be able to shoot for some time.
♦ Goes under the water, charges at the player and leaves a poisounous creep on the ground.
♦ Fires two guns at the same time starting from her had down to her hips, this happens back to back 2 times.
♦ Shoots a ring of bullets in every direction with little gaps, this gaps will close at the end.
